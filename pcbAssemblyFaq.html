PCB ASSEMBLY FAQ
Q: Who is PCBGOGO.com?
PCBGOGO is a PCB manufacturing and assembly services manufacturer from Shenzhen, China. It provides quick turn PCB fabrication and fast PCB assembly services to our clients.
Q: Do you accept to solder the boards that are not made by PCBGOGO.com?
Accepted.
Q: What files and documents do you request for my PCBA orders?
PCB file, BOM list, pick and place file (centroid file).
Q: What is centroid?
Centroid data file is a specific file containing all the required information used for placing the surface mount components onto the circuit board. This is also knows as insertion, pick & place, or XY data. Some of the CAD tools will automatically generate this file and some may not, but you may need to modify this file and then generate the centroid file. The centroid file describes the position and orientation of all the surface mount parts, which includes the reference designator, X and Y position, rotation and side of board (top or bottom). Only surface mounting parts are listed in the centroid.
Q: How do you request for the shipping and marking of the parts for the consigned or kitted order?
We hope you can mark on any individual package or box with the corresponding line number on BOM, manufacturers’ part# or customers’ part#, quantities. A detailed packing list is preferred to enable us to count and check upon receipt of your parts..
Q: Do you offer turnkey PCB assembly service?
Surely 100%
Q: Do you assemble BGA? Are there any special requirements for this?
Yes, we have the ability to handle BGA of 0.25mm pitch with X-ray testing. For BGA, we need to tent any vias under the BGA with solder mask to ensure the soldering quality.
Q: How do you quote the total price of PCBA service?
Online quote is only for PCB assembly fee. If you need turnkey PCBA service, we will quote your PCB cost, PCB assembly fee, and components with shipping freight as total amount.
Q: When does the turn-time begin?
Determining the turn-time of PCB services before the parts are collected is quite complex and is not accurate. Therefore our policy is that turn-time begins immediately after all the parts and PCBs are ready and all the PCB files (Gerber files/other PCB files, etc.) centroid (pick & place PNP file, XY data or files in other formats), BOM, and all other necessary data or documents/images/photos are complete for our assembly work.
Q: 10. Is your assembly RoHS compliant?
Yes, but we also offer leaded PCBA services.
Q: What are your PCB assembly standards?
IPC-A-610 Class 2.
