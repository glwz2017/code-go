HOW TO PLACE A ASSEMBLY ORDER

PCBGOGO printed circuit board assembly services for prototypes is based on a 2-4 working day delivery term. If the quantity of boards and/or the number of components is above a certain level, then this may change to 7 or 10 working days. Expedited soldering service can be offered as 24 hours. The actual terms offered are completely transparent and available on completion of our online ‘Instant quote calculator’.

The Purchase and Logistics of the components happens during the lead time of the PCB production. Only when the PCB’s are ready and components available does the population service and Delivery terms start.

This guide is for customers who is going to place the order on PCBGOGO website..

To Sign In or Register for an account please click here.

How does it work?

Firstly, Select the PCB Assembly calculator from the PCBGOGO the below Page or within your registered account then select option “PCB Assembly”. This option is also available through the PCBGOGO navigation bar.

https://www.pcbgogo.com/pcb-assembly.html

Secondly, fill in all data in calculator system and press “CALCULATE”. Please pay attention to the 4 important options which explanation is below:
Number of PCB: the total quantity of PCBs.
Number of Unique Parts: the total quantity of components for each board.
Number of SMD Parts: the total quantity of SMD components for each board.
Number of Through-Hole Parts: the total quantity of THT components for each board.
Furthermore, you could write special requirement for your assembly in “ Detailed information of assembly”.

Thirdly, after you press “CALCULATE” button, the price will come out. But price is only for assembly and shipping freight, components cost is not included. So, if you need the complete quotation, you should write the email address and click” ADD TO BASKET” (If you are a new comer, you should register first, then log in.)

Next, you need to upload the PCB file, BOM list and pick & place file to the order. Our PCB assembly engineer will review the files within 48 hours and update the accurate price in the order. At last, the sales will send the complete quotation to your email. Finally, if you confirm the quotation, please make payment and it will be successfully to place an assembly order in PCBGOGO.
