Notice of Resuming operations &
Delivery Delay due to Coronavirus (Feb. 24st)
Since the Corona virus outbreak in Wuhan, China. The Chinese government has taken the strictest measures to prevent the spread of the epidemic and guarantee the safety of the people, some of the employees are unable to resume work due to 14 days self-quarantine and government approval, thus the orders are not time guaranteed at this time. The situation is better now and we have started working home and factories opened already since Feb.10th.
Resuming operations as following
a. 2-layer and 4-layer PCB prototype and small volume PCB fabrication, as well as stencil have been resumed.
b. PCB assembly orders have been resumed on Feb. 12th.
c. HDI and FPC/Rigid Flex orders will be resumed on Feb. 22nd. c.HDI and FPC/Rigid-Flex orders have been resumed on Feb.22nd.

Notes

1. The lead time of multi-layer(4 and more layers) PCB orders needs extra 15 days and lead time of 1-2 layers PCB orders need extra 10 days. And other specifications should be as follows:
√  Solder Mask: Green;
√  The thickness of PCB: 1.0mm, 1.2mm, 1.6mm, 2.0mm, 2.4mm;
√  Surface Finish: HASL with lead, HASL lead free, and immersion gold (ENIG);
√  Orders within 1㎡ or within 100pcs PCBs.
2. The lead time of other orders need extra 20 days based on the normal delivery.
3. SMT assembly orders will be arranged according to your payment time.
4. Fast-delivery orders are still available, and we will prioritize expedited orders, but it will be delayed due to the current situation.
5. DHL and SF Express have resumed operation, FedEx has stopped picking up packages in China, service time need further notice.

We’re trying our best to process your orders, we hope to get your kindly understanding if there caused any delay due to the current situation, sorry for the inconvenience. Any update will be posted on our website and SNS platform, thank you for your patience and support on us.

Feel free to contact us via email service@pcbgogo.com, we will reply you as soon as possible.
