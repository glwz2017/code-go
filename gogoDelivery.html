Shipping process

After production and testing, Your PCB orders will be sent to our shipping department. As the quick turn PCB manufacturer, PCBGOGO shipping department will ship your PCB quickly without any pending. For example, the 24 hours express PCB order will be shipped out in 24 hours after it is fabricated. So the 48 hour express does.

How to ship the goods?

Firstly, PCBGOGO shipping department will print order address and invoice in PCBGOGO website system.
Secondly, PCBGOGO will set the shipment information on the DHL website.
Thirdly, the DHL staff will collect the package from PCBGOGO and ship it to you.

Shipping package.

To avoid shipping damage, we will pack our PCBs in several steps by difference package material.

● Firstly, the PCB order will be packed by vacuum bubbles bag.
● Secondly, after vacuum package it will be packed at our special carton box. We have designed several special carton boxes in difference size to pack and ship the PCB orders.
● At last, the package will be packed by shipping company bag or others wrapper.
