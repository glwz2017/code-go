INTRODUCTION

ShenZhen PCBgogo

PCBGOGO has over ten years’ experience in PCB fabrication and assembly, highly specialized not only in quick-turn PCB prototype and PCB assembly, but also medium and mass volume PCB fabrication. We have two factories, over 2400 sqm, fully compliant with the ISO 9001:2008 quality management system, UL certificate and are committed to adhering to the strictest standards in manufacture and assembly. Since 2015, PCBGOGO has delivered over US$1000,000 .

worth of electronic components and prototyping services to more than 20000 customers all over the world.

“Full refund will be offered if there has any problem in PCB and assembly quality which could not be solved!” our COO, Eric Wu said. Long time business cooperation is PCBGOGO’s goal. Even if the price is not cheapest, quality and service will be best.

Our advantages:

We offer low cost with high quality PCB prototype and PCB assembly service, minimum order is 5pieces.

Product quality guaranteed. We fully compliant with the ISO 9001:2008 quality management system and UL certificate. All of the circuit boards will be tested well before shipped out.

Instant online quote. Our price structure is transparent and has no any hidden cost, you will know where your money is going.

PCB build time as fast as 2-3days and can provide express 12hours& 24hours service if your project is urgent.

Various shipping methods are available, such as DHL, UPS, EMS, FedEx and HK post.

You can track your PCB and assembly order status online. You will be informed of the every manufacture process and the shipping status of your order.
